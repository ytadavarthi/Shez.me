Who I Am
and What I Do.

I'm Yasasvi (aka Shez), a highly motivated dude with a penchant for coding and medicine. Working with both doctors and software developers, I noticed first-hand the rift caused between the workflow of a physician and how developers may imagine it to be.

I want to help.

My focus is to create the best user experience possible for any project that I do. To truly help further medicine, I think it is important that everything be intuitive and quick. I'm both a designer and a medical student, and with my eye for detail, I know that I can make a positive impact.
